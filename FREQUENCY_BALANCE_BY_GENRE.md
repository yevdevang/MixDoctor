### **Acceptable Variations:**
- Frequency percentages can vary Â±5% within genre guidelines
- Creative choices may intentionally deviate from these standards
- Mastering choices depend on target playback systems

### **Red Flags:**
- **Low End >70%:** Likely to cause mud and translation issues
- **High frequencies <2%:** May sound dull or overly filtered
- **Midrange <15%:** Vocals and lead instruments may lack presence
- **Any band >80%:** Extreme imbalance affecting overall clarity

### **Context Considerations:**
- **Streaming platforms:** May require more conservative low end
- **Club/dance music:** Can handle more extreme low end emphasis
- **Radio play:** Benefits from enhanced midrange presence
- **Audiophile releases:** May favor more balanced distribution
